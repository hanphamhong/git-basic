About Me


I began my career in hospitality management, where I focused on designing seamless guest experiences and optimizing revenue strategies. As I delved deeper into pricing models, customer feedback, and operational performance, I discovered a passion that went beyond daily service—the creative challenge of using data to solve complex business problems. That curiosity led me to pursue Business Analytics and International Business, where I now explore how data drives smarter decisions across industries. Along the way, I’ve built projects ranging from asset management database optimization to cryptocurrency price forecasting and tourism strategy modeling—each one a chance to combine analytical thinking with creativity. My technical toolkit includes SQL, Python, Tableau, and Power BI, but what truly drives me is the opportunity to uncover patterns, ask the right questions, and design data solutions that inspire action and innovation.
